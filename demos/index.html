FAQs

Straight answers

Practical questions about how this works — no fluff. If your question isn't here, ask it on the call.

Getting started
How quickly can someone start?
+
Most placements are live within 5–7 working days of the discovery call. The timeline depends on the role complexity and your onboarding availability. We handle screening and shortlisting — you interview the final two or three candidates.
Do you work with our existing software?
+
Yes. We place VAs who are experienced with tools commonly used in UK/Irish construction: Procore, Aconex, Viewpoint, AutoCAD, BIM 360, Microsoft 365, Xero, and others. We confirm tool fit during the discovery call.
Commercials
How is pricing structured?
+
We charge a fixed weekly retainer based on agreed hours. There are no recruitment fees on top — the placement and ongoing support is included. Rates are agreed on a call after we understand your requirements.
Is there a minimum commitment?
+
We ask for a 4-week initial period to allow for proper onboarding and workflow establishment. After that, arrangements are rolling with notice on either side. We don't lock you into long-term contracts.
Delivery
What if it's not the right fit?
+
We handle a replacement at no additional cost if the VA isn't working out within the first 30 days. After that, we work with you to diagnose the issue — sometimes it's a workflow or communication adjustment rather than a personnel change.
Where are the VAs based and what hours do they work?
+
Primarily the Philippines. We structure their working hours to overlap with UK/Ireland business hours (typically 8am–4pm or 9am–5pm GMT/BST) so your team can communicate in real time during the core site day.
