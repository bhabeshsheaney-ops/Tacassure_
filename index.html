Who we are

Taca (support) + Assure (guarantee) = Tacassure.

Tacassure is a venture by Sheaney Ltd., designed to bridge the skills gap in the construction and engineering sectors across the United Kingdom (UK) and the Republic of Ireland (ROI) by providing highly skilled remote virtual assistants (VAs).

Founded by Steven Heaney (Managing Director) and Brian Cruz (Operations Manager), we tackle labour shortages and control costs with VAs renowned for their excellent English proficiency, technical expertise, and cultural adaptability.

The global VA market is projected to grow at a 24%+ CAGR through 2029, with construction-specialist VAs in particularly high demand.

What we cover

Roles we place — by category

Core support categories we place for construction and engineering teams across the UK and ROI.

Operations & Administration

Timesheets, email triage, scheduling, and filing. With a VA, your calendar runs smoothly, emails get prioritised, and nothing gets lost.

Tender & Proposal Management

When tenders come in, your VA manages the chaos; compiling specs, formatting documents, and tracking deadlines.

Client Correspondence

Emails pile up. Photos scatter. Your VA becomes your client interface: logging queries, organising documentation, drafting updates, and flagging issues.

Project Coordination & Scheduling

Your VA synchronises everyone and everything by managing meetings, tracking action items, and following up on deliverables.

Procurement & Supplier Coordination

Your VA tracks all quotations, submittals, specs, and delivery dates. They verify compliance and keep the pipeline moving.

Quality Checks & Documentation Review

Your VA reviews completeness, flags issues, and prepares QA reports. Mistakes are caught early and your reputation is protected.

Data Management & Reporting

Your VA organises site photos, drawings, specs, and timesheets, then compiles monthly reports. You find what you need without asking.

Financial Administration

Your VA manages invoices, purchase orders, expenses, and budgets. Improved visibility. Better control. Cleaner year-end records.

ISO & Compliance Management

ISO compliance is one less headache. Your VA maintains documentation, tracks corrective actions, and prepares you for the next audit.
